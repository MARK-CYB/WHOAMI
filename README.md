### 🛡️ Security Philosophy
I believe that effective security is not just about identifying vulnerabilities, but understanding the underlying logic of a system to systematically improve its resilience. My approach mirrors professional pentesting lifecycles:
*   **Reconnaissance & Enumeration:** Establishing a baseline and mapping the attack surface.
*   **Vulnerability Assessment:** Identifying misconfigurations and security flaws through methodical analysis.
*   **Exploitation & Post-Exploitation:** Testing boundaries to demonstrate real-world risk.
*   **Documentation & Reporting:** Articulating findings clearly to drive remediation.
